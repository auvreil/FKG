About

Established by
operators, for operators.

Front Kellner Group is a private contractor organisation structured along conventional military lines and held to corresponding professional standards. We do not expand for expansion's sake. Our mandate is the delivery of effective, coordinated capability across ground, reconnaissance, and support fuctions. All while executed with discipline regardless of conditions.

Personnel advance through demonstrated performance and professional character. Tenure is not a qualification. Every position within FKG carries defined responsibilities and is expected to be filled accordingly.

Candidates who understand the distinction between an organisation and a community, and who are prepared to operate within the former, are encouraged to proceed.

Requirements

Candidate
Standards.

Selection into FKG is not predicated on prior experience or technical skill. It is predicated on attitude, reliability, and readiness to operate within a structured, doctrine-driven environment. The following criteria apply uniformly to all candidates, without exception.

  1. 01
    Age

    Minimum 21 years of age. No exceptions. FKG operates as a professional organisation and requires all personnel to meet the threshold for adult & legal accountability.

  2. 02
    Communications

    Clear, composed communication is a non-negotiable operational requirement. Candidates must be capable of transmitting and receiving information accurately under pressure, using concise, unambiguous language.

  3. 03
    Availability & Operational Tempo

    Members are expected to maintain an availability posture consistent with their assigned element's operational schedule. Planned unavailability must be communicated in advance through the appropriate chain of command. Persistent non-availability is grounds for administrative review.

  4. 04
    Conduct

    Professional conduct is expected at all times, on and off operation. Dissent is managed through appropriate channels. Conduct that degrades operational effectiveness, unit cohesion, or the professional standing of FKG will not be tolerated and is subject to immediate administrative action.

  5. 05
    Professional Development

    Prior operational experience is recognised but not required. What is required is a genuine commitment to ongoing development — tactically, procedurally, and interpersonally. FKG invests in its personnel; personnel are expected to invest in themselves.

  6. 06
    Probationary Period

    All incoming personnel serve a structured four-week candidate evaluation period prior to confirmed assignment. This phase is mutual: candidates assess FKG's environment and culture while the organisation assesses theirs.
