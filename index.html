About

Built on standards,
not spectacle.

Front Kellner Group was founded on a simple conviction: that fighting is most meaningful when it demands something of its participants. We do not chase novelty or numbers. We build toward cohesion, a unit that operates with clarity under pressure and holds itself to a standard whether or not anyone is watching.

Every member is both student and contributor. Rank is earned through demonstrated competence and character, not tenure.

If you are looking for an organization that takes the craft seriously, reads the map, and trusts its people - this is the place to find it.

Requirements

What we ask
of every applicant.

Membership in FKG is not selective for skill. It is selective for attitude, reliability, and commitment. The following standards apply uniformly to all candidates.

  1. 01
    Age

    Minimum 18 years of age. No exceptions. We operate as adults and expect adult conduct.

  2. 02
    Communications

    The ability to communicate clearly and calmly under pressure. Good communication is a key to all operations.

  3. 03
    Attendance

    Members are expected to attend a minimum of an operation once per 3 years. Planned absences must be communicated in advance through the appropriate channels.

  4. 04
    Conduct

    Professional behaviour at all times. Dissent is welcome, disrespect is not. FKG has zero tolerance for conduct that degrades the operational environment or its members.

  5. 05
    Willingness to Learn

    Prior experience in conflict is appreciated but not required. A genuine interest in improving tactically, procedurally, and interpersonally, is non-negotiable.

  6. 06
    Probationary Period

    All new members serve a structured four-week probationary period. This is a mutual evaluation, you assess us as much as we assess you.
