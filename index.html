About

Structure before
everything else.

Front Kellner Group is a private contractor organisation structured along conventional military lines. We hold ourselves to professional standards equivalent to that and conduct ourselves accordingly, regardless of the environment or conditions present.

We do not expand for the sake of it. Capability is built incrementally and personnel are assessed on performance, not tenure. Every role within FKG carries defined responsibilities and genuine accountability.

We maintain an active presence on YouTube and X, where we document select operations and training activities. That said, intake remains selective. Candidates are expected to have reviewed what we do and to have a clear reason for applying before making contact.

If you understand the difference between an organisation and a community, and are prepared to operate within the former, you are welcome to proceed.

Requirements

Candidate
Standards.

Selection is not predicated on prior experience or technical background. It is predicated on reliability, composure, and the ability to function within a structured environment without requiring constant direction. These criteria apply without exception.

  1. 01
    Age

    Minimum 21 years of age, no exceptions. FKG operates as a professional organisation and all personnel are expected to meet the threshold for full adult and legal accountability in their jurisdiction.

  2. 02
    Firearms Certification

    All candidates must hold a valid firearms licence or certification applicable to their jurisdiction prior to consideration. FKG does not accept candidates who are unlicensed to handle the weapons systems used in our operations. Proof of certification is required at application stage and must remain current throughout service.

  3. 03
    Communications

    Accurate, composed communication under pressure is an operational requirement, not a preference. Candidates must be capable of transmitting and receiving information clearly and concisely, without ambiguity.

  4. 04
    Availability

    Personnel are expected to maintain availability consistent with their element's operational schedule. Planned absence must be communicated in advance through the chain of command. Persistent unavailability is grounds for administrative review.

  5. 05
    Conduct

    Professional conduct is expected at all times, on and off operation. Dissent is managed through appropriate channels. Conduct that degrades operational effectiveness, unit cohesion, or the professional standing of FKG is subject to immediate administrative action.

  6. 06
    Professional Development

    Prior operational experience is recognised where it exists and is not required. What is required is a genuine and sustained commitment to development, tactically, procedurally, and interpersonally. FKG invests in its personnel. Personnel are expected to invest in themselves.

  7. 07
    Evaluation Period

    All incoming personnel complete a structured four-week evaluation prior to confirmed assignment. The period is mutual. Candidates assess the organisation and the organisation assesses the candidate. A formal offer of assignment is not guaranteed at the end of it.

How to Apply

Intake procedure.

I

Review

Read the candidate standards in full. If you meet the criteria and understand what is being asked of you, proceed to contact.

II

Initial Contact

Submit an expression of interest to fkg.upfront734@passinbox.com. Keep it direct. State your background, your certifications, and which element you are applying for. We do not respond to vague or generic inquiries.

III

Formal Application

Shortlisted candidates receive a formal application package. Complete it in full. Incomplete submissions are not followed up on.

IV

Assessment

Approved candidates are integrated into a supervised tasking as part of the evaluation period. A formal offer of assignment is extended only upon satisfactory completion.
